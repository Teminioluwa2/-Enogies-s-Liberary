Few weeks after I started dating Rae , I got admission into the University of Lagos . I was excited because it was my dream school. I called Rae to tell him. He was sad and I understood his point. "Babe , don't you think it's time we meet??" Rae asked when I called him "Yes, I thought of that" I replied sharply. So we fixed our meeting date on a Saturday. I took permission from my mom and she allowed buh insisted I come back home on time. I was set . I wore a pink gown. I got to the venue late. By the time I got there , Rae and his cousin brother Tim were seated. "Sorry I came late, I had difficulty locating this place" I quickly apologized. "No qualms" Tim spoke So he spoke slangs. I was surprised because here in Nigeria slangs are mainly for touts. "My manners , please have your seat" Rae offered me a seat next to him. Tim left us. "You are beautiful" Rae whispered in my ears. "Thanks" I managed to say. We chatted about so many things. I told him I wanted to go home. We left. I spent more time with Rae since I was going to school soon . We spent more days together. One evening , Rae called me phone. " Babe, I need to tell you something very important" , he said. " Buh I just left your hux" I complained. "It's urgent".....he started coughing "I'm on my way" I ended the call and started running to his house I got to his house and met him watching movie. He could be tricky sometimes I thought. "Rae , you scared me" I said " Sorry I did. I want to tell you something . "What that"I asked "Hmm..... I know you will be going to school anytime from now. You know when most Nigeria girls go to the university they forget about their boyfriend. I don't want our case to be like that. "Wifey, That was the name Tim normally calls me. He noticed I wasn't smiling. "Did you two have a fight" he asked "No" I said crying now. Tim hugged me and took me back inside. "I'm sorry Ella" Rae managed to say Tim said so many things and I laughed. "Why don't you spend the night with us" Tim said. "Hmm....okkk. let me call my mom" I sighed. I took my phone and called my mom telling her I won't be around. She didn't complain. She just told me to come back home on time the next day. "Thanks Mom" I shouted on my phone "Rare gem" I whispered as I dropped my phone.
